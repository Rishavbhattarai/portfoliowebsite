Web Development

I took an active involvement in the creation of the Pay Me and AwiPay digital wallet apps. I was instrumental in developing functionality that meet particular client requirements by leveraging Java Spring Boot. I put in place mechanisms that let users make up to three transactions a day using linked cards, but only allowed one transaction per day for the wallet itself in order to ensure safe and controlled spending. In addition, I helped to provide a seamless and effective customer experience by contributing to the processing of transactions with third-party merchants.

Android Development

During my tenure as an Android developer, I contributed significantly to the development of the AwiPay digital wallet app. I made use of my knowledge of Kotlin and Java to add to the essential features of the program. In addition to working on the UI, I also tested and developed the online applications and APIs that the Android application smoothly integrates with. I now have a comprehensive understanding of the whole Android app development cycle thanks to this experience, which will help to make sure AwiPay users have a reliable and easy-to-use experience.
